There are many interconnected features and technologies in Taskwarrior, Taskserver, Tasksh and Timewarrior, each piece having it's own goals.

This matrix allows a simple reading of where things are, and where they are going. This is a low-resolution time line. It is subject to change. It does not constitute a concrete plan. This is an all-volunteer effort, and scheduling is difficult.
