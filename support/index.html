Taskwarrior is supported by volunteers, in these channels.

Email

You can ask for help at support@tasktools.org. We request that you first look around at the full online documentation first, to give yourself a chance to answer your own question.

IRC

There is an active and well-attended #taskwarrior channel on freenode.

Documentation

There is full online documentation to help you diagnose problem, or learn about features and usage.

  • Take a look at the troubleshooting sync guide.

Issue Tracking

Use our issue tracker to see if your problem is reported, fixed, or needs to be reported. Perhaps you have a clarifying example? Perhaps vote on a feature? Insight into a reported problem? Want to request a feature or improvement?

Development

Are you a developer? You might be looking for other tools, continuous integration reports, or beta downloads. Come on over to our workshop at tasktools.org
