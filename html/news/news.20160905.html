Activity Digest: August 2016 2016-09-01

This is an ongoing series of activity reports, published monthly, to highlight activity in our projects. Here is what happened in August 2016. This is not a complete list of all activity, just work that results in a non-trivial change. For a full list, see the git history of all the projects.

Summary

August saw a lot of documentation updates and preparation for the release of Timewarrior 1.0.0 and FrOSCon 2016. Focus then shifted to finishing up Tasksh 1.1.0 work.
